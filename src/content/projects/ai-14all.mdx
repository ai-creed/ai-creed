## what it does

ai-14all keeps the active worktree, shell sessions, code inspection, and lightweight git review in one place without turning into a full IDE. It is a small desktop app for engineers who live in the terminal but want faster context-switching across parallel work branches.

## why

Worktree flows mean juggling multiple shells, editors, and review tabs. 14all collapses that into one window — one shell per worktree, one click to review, no ceremony.
