## what it does

ai-whisper is a local bridge that lets two agent sessions collaborate: one relays work to the other via inline commands, they trade the baton, and a supervising evaluator watches the round trip.

## why

Hard agent tasks go better with a second agent reviewing. Whisper makes that pairing feel like one workflow instead of two terminals pretending to talk to each other.
